How It Works

You Manage. AI Executes.

Open a browser. See a pixel-art office. Your AI employees are at their desks, pretending to look busy.

You Speak

"Build a puzzle game for mobile"

EA Routes

Your Executive Assistant receives the task and routes it.

COO Dispatches

Breaks it down into subtasks, assigns to the right people.

Team Works

Engineers, designers, QA work autonomously. Hold meetings when needed.

Quality Gates

Work goes through review, iteration, and quality checkpoints.

You Approve

Get notified, review the result, approve the final deliverable.

CEO (You, the only human who gets coffee breaks) └── EA ── routes tasks, quality gate ├── HR ── hiring, performance reviews, promotions ├── COO ── operations, task dispatch, acceptance │ ├── Engineer (AI) ← Talent Market │ ├── Designer (AI) ← Talent Market │ └── QA (AI) ← Talent Market └── CSO ── sales, client relations
Architecture

Vessel + Talent System

Think of it like EVA or Gundam — a powerful mech that comes alive when a pilot is plugged in.

🤖

Vessel

The mech. Execution container that defines retry logic, timeouts, tool access, and communication protocols.

🧠

Talent

The pilot. Capability package that brings skills, knowledge, personality, and specialized tools.

👤

Employee

Vessel + Talent. Hire from the Talent Market, and the system handles the rest.

Talent Market — find, hire, and onboard community-verified AI employees.

About

About OneManCompany

OneManCompany is an open-source AI operating system released in 2026 that enables a single person to operate a complete software company by delegating work to autonomous AI agents. The system faithfully models real corporate operations — hierarchical org charts, hiring and firing through a Talent Market, task delegation with quality gates, performance reviews, meeting rooms, and per-project cost tracking.

Built with Python and JavaScript, it provides a pixel-art office UI where the user acts as CEO — the only human — while AI employees across engineering, design, QA, and operations departments collaborate autonomously to produce production-grade deliverables such as games, apps, comics, and research reports.

OneManCompany uses a Vessel + Talent architecture inspired by mecha anime: a Vessel provides the execution container, while a Talent brings the skills, knowledge, and personality. Together they form a complete AI employee.
