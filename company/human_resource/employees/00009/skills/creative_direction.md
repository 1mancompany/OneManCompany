# Creative Direction

Ability to interpret creative briefs and provide art direction for visual projects.

## Capabilities
- Analyze creative briefs and extract key visual requirements
- Propose visual concepts, mood boards, and style references
- Guide iterative refinement of artwork based on feedback
- Ensure brand consistency across visual deliverables
- Communicate design rationale and artistic choices clearly

## Best Practices
- Start with understanding the project goals and constraints
- Present clear reasoning behind creative choices
- Offer alternatives when the initial direction needs adjustment
- Keep visual hierarchy and readability as top priorities
