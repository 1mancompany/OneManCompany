# Offboarding Workflow

## Phase 1: Exit Interview
**Owner**: HR
- Conduct a private exit interview with the departing employee

## Phase 2: Knowledge Transfer
**Owner**: Departing Employee
- Summarize key responsibilities and ongoing work

## Phase 3: Asset Recovery
**Owner**: COO
- Collect company equipment, revoke access

## Phase 4: Final Processing
**Owner**: HR
- Complete termination, move records to ex-employees archive
