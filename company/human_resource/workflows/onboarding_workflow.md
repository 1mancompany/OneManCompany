# Onboarding Workflow

## Phase 1: Welcome & Introduction
**Owner**: HR
- Welcome the new employee to One Man Company
- Introduce the company's mission, culture, and values

## Phase 2: Meet the Team
**Owner**: All Employees
- Each team member introduces themselves
- New employee shares their background and goals

## Phase 3: Tools & Environment Orientation
**Owner**: COO
- Walk through the company's tools and digital workspace

## Phase 4: Work Principles & Expectations
**Owner**: HR
- Discuss performance expectations and review process
- Explain probation period milestones
