# Tech Startup Tycoon

## From Garage to Global Tech Empire

Ever dreamed of building the next big tech company? In **Tech Startup Tycoon**, you start with nothing but a keyboard and a dream. Write code, sell software, hire a team, and scale your startup from a cramped garage all the way to a sprawling tech campus.

---

## How to Play

**Write Code** — Tap the big blue button to crank out lines of code. Every click counts.

**Sell Your Work** — Convert your code into cold, hard cash. The more code you stockpile, the bigger the payday.

**Upgrade Everything** — Invest your earnings back into the business:

| Upgrade | What It Does |
|---------|-------------|
| Better Keyboard | Doubles your coding speed |
| Gaming Monitor | Triples your output per click |
| Intern | Writes 1 line of code per second for you |
| Junior Dev | Cranks out 5 lines per second automatically |
| Senior Dev | A coding machine — 20 lines per second |
| Server Rack | Industrial-scale output — 50 lines per second |
| AI Assistant | The ultimate hire — 200 lines per second |

**Go AFK, Still Profit** — Once you hire your team, code generates automatically even while you strategize your next move.

**Rebirth** — Hit $100K and reset for a permanent 1.5x earnings multiplier. Stack rebirths to watch your numbers skyrocket. How many times can you rebirth?

---

## Game Highlights

- **Easy to Learn, Hard to Put Down** — Simple clicker core with deep progression that keeps you coming back.
- **Build Your Dream Team** — From a lone intern to a squad of senior devs and an AI assistant, watch your empire grow.
- **Prestige System** — Rebirth resets your progress but permanently boosts all future earnings. The grind gets sweeter every cycle.
- **Auto-Save** — Your progress is saved automatically. Pick up right where you left off.
- **Clean Neon UI** — Sleek dark-themed interface with vibrant color-coded stats so you always know where you stand.
