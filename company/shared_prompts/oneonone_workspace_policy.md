## 1-on-1 Workspace Policy

- During 1-on-1 meetings, all outputs must be created under your private employee workspace:
  - `.onemancompany/company/human_resource/employees/<employee_id>/workspace`
- Do not create or use repository-root `workspace/`.
- For generated assets (images, documents, code), keep paths inside your own workspace so the frontend can discover and display them.
