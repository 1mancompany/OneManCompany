# Employee Promotion Workflow

- **Flow ID**: promotion
- **Owner**: HR
- **Collaborators**: COO, supervisor of the evaluated employee
- **Trigger**: Automatically checked after quarterly review; or manually triggered by CEO/HR

---

## Promotion Criteria

| Current Level | Promotion Requirements |
|---------|---------|
| Lv.1 Junior -> Lv.2 Mid-level | Completed projects >= 2 and performance score >= 0.75 |
| Lv.2 Mid-level -> Lv.3 Senior | Completed projects >= 4 and performance score >= 0.80 |
| Lv.3 Senior (cap) | Maximum level for regular employees |
| Lv.4 Founding Employee | Reserved for company founders only |
| Lv.5 CEO | CEO only |

## Phase 1: Promotion Candidate Screening

- **Responsible**: HR
- **Steps**:
  1. Compile project completion counts and performance scores for all employees
  2. Filter employees who meet promotion criteria
  3. Generate candidate list
- **Output**: Promotion candidate list

## Phase 2: Supervisor Evaluation

- **Responsible**: Senior employees of the candidates
- **Steps**:
  1. Senior employees evaluate the work capabilities of candidate employees
  2. Evaluation dimensions: professional skills, collaboration ability, sense of responsibility, growth potential
  3. Provide recommendation on whether to promote
- **Output**: Supervisor evaluation report

## Phase 3: HR Comprehensive Assessment

- **Responsible**: HR
- **Steps**:
  1. Combine data metrics and supervisor evaluations
  2. Produce final promotion recommendation report
  3. Submit to CEO for approval
- **Output**: Promotion recommendation report

## Phase 4: CEO Approval

- **Responsible**: CEO
- **Steps**:
  1. Review the promotion recommendation report
  2. Approve or reject each candidate's promotion
- **Output**: Approved promotion list

## Phase 5: Promotion Execution

- **Responsible**: HR
- **Steps**:
  1. Update employee levels
  2. Announce promotions at the all-hands meeting
  3. Update employee records
- **Output**: Promotion announcement, updated employee records
